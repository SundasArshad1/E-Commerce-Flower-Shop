Fresh flower care

Does a bouquet of flower needs sunlight?
Cut flowers do not need sunlight to survive, in fact keeping them directly in front of a window can actually dry them out! Since they've already been cut away from the main plant,you can put them anywhere in your home. Although, they like it better away from direct air conditioning or heating.
How long does a bouquet of flower last without water ?
We recommend that your flowers should not be left out of water any longer than an hour. And always remember to cut the stems before they return to water.The base stem cells dry out within 10 mins, but love a chop and will rehydrate very well within the next hour.
