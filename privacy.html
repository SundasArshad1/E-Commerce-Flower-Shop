PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

Welcome to ORCHID! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you of the personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data when you register for an account, place an order, or contact us. The personal data we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, and address.

How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal data to process your orders, communicate with you, and improve our services. We may also use your data for marketing purposes, but only if you have given us your consent.

Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share your personal data with third-party service providers who help us with our business operations, such as payment processing and shipping. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. You can also object to the processing of your personal data or request that we restrict its processing. If you have any questions or concerns about your personal data, please contact us at privacy@orchid.com.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
